Automation

The server schedule works while the browser is closed. At each due time it checks feeds. If AI updates are enabled below, it then evaluates ready entries, publishes affected summaries, and finally sends duplicate-safe ntfy device alerts.

Model & writing

These settings govern ordinary feed summaries. New settings are frozen at the start of the next update. Successful existing scores are retained; only the summary composition is rebuilt from current eligible evidence. The separate arXiv digest has its own recipe.
