What happens next

  1. Configuration, subscriptions, database, plugin state, and any stored secrets are prepared together in a private staging folder.
  2. DistillFeed verifies the complete staged reader.
  3. Only a verified reader becomes active. A failure leaves no half-installed reader.

Setup guarantees
